Thai Coconut Noodle Soup

Creamy, aromatic, and surprisingly mild. Perfect for introducing Asian flavors.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can coconut milk (13.5 oz)
  • 1 tablespoon red curry paste (mild)
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 8 oz rice noodles
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables
  • 1 can baby corn (8 oz)
  • Fresh basil & cilantro
  • Lime wedges

Instructions

  1. 1
    Make the Broth

    Heat broth and coconut milk. Stir in curry paste, fish sauce, lime juice, and sugar. Simmer gently.

  2. 2
    Add Vegetables

    Add carrots first (5 minutes), then snap peas, bell pepper, and baby corn. Simmer 5 more minutes.

  3. 3
    Cook Noodles

    Cook rice noodles per package directions. Drain and rinse.

  4. 4
    Serve with Joy

    Place noodles in bowls. Ladle broth and veggies on top. Garnish with basil and cilantro. Squeeze lime!
