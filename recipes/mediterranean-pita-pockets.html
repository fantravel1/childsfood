Mediterranean Pita Pockets

Fresh, colorful, and fully customizable. Kids love building their own!

Ingredients

  • 4 whole wheat pita pockets
  • 2 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1 cup diced feta cheese
  • 1 cup kalamata olives (optional)
  • 1 cup tzatziki sauce
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. 1
    Prepare Veggies

    Wash and slice all vegetables. Keep everything fresh and colorful on the counter.

  2. 2
    Make Tzatziki Mix

    Combine tzatziki, fresh dill, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Taste and adjust to preference.

  3. 3
    Warm Pitas

    Warm pitas in the oven at 350¬∞F for 3 minutes. Warm pitas are easier to stuff without tearing.

  4. 4
    Build Together

    Let kids assemble their own! Spread tzatziki, then layer greens, cucumber, tomatoes, onion, feta, and olives.

  5. 5
    Serve Fresh

    Serve immediately with extra tzatziki for dipping. Fresh is best!

  • Build Their Own: This is the ultimate kid-friendly recipe‚Äîthey control every layer
  • Color Rainbow: "Can you use every color of the rainbow in your pocket?"
  • Tasting Bar: Put each veggie in a separate bowl‚Äîtaste first, then choose what goes in
  • Feta Preference: Some kids love it, some don't. Have it available but not mandatory
  • Sauce Control: Let them control how much tzatziki goes in their pocket

üåü Why Kids Love This

These are totally customizable. Every kid can build exactly what they want, which builds tremendous confidence and joy around eating. The pita pocket is fun to hold, the tzatziki is creamy and tangy, and the veggies are fresh and crunchy.

Perfect for Picky Eaters: They can choose which veggies to include. No hidden ingredients. Complete transparency builds trust with food.
