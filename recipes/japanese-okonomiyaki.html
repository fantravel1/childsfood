Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1/4 cup diced green onions
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoons okonomiyaki sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Japanese mayo
  • Aonori (seaweed powder)
  • Bonito flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. 1. Make Batter

    Whisk flour, water, and eggs. Keep it slightly lumpy. Add salt and pepper.

  2. 2. Add Vegetables

    Fold in cabbage and green onions gently. Don't overmix.

  3. 3. Cook Pancakes

    Heat oil in a pan. Pour batter into circles, flatten slightly. Cook 4-5 minutes per side until golden.

  4. 4. Top & Serve

    Drizzle with sauce and mayo. Sprinkle aonori. Watch bonito flakes dance!
