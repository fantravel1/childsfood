Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground lamb/beef
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley
  • Salt, pepper, oil
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt

Instructions

  1. 1. Mix meat, breadcrumbs, egg, garlic, spices, parsley.
  2. 2. Form into balls. Heat oil in pan.
  3. 3. Brown meatballs 10 min, turning often.
  4. 4. Serve with yogurt sauce & pita bread!
