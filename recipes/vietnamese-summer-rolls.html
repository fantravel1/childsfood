Ingredients

  • 8 rice paper wrappers
  • 2 cups shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 cup cooked vermicelli
  • Fresh mint & cilantro
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce

Instructions

  1. 1. Make sauce: Whisk peanut butter, water, honey, soy sauce until smooth.
  2. 2. Prep: Arrange veggies, noodles, herbs in separate bowls.
  3. 3. Soak rice paper in warm water 5 seconds. Lay flat.
  4. 4. Layer and roll: lettuce, veggies, noodles, herbs. Fold sides, roll tight. Dip in sauce!

Why Kids Love This

It's a DIY meal! Kids love rolling their own, choosing their fillings, and dipping. Fresh, light, and fun‚Äîplus they're eating tons of veggies.
