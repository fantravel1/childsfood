Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti or penne
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes (28 oz)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh basil (optional)
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  2. 2Saut√© garlic: Heat oil, add garlic, cook 1 minute until fragrant.

  5. 5Combine: Toss pasta with sauce. Top with basil and parmesan. Done!

Why Kids Love This

This is simplicity at its best. Pasta is always a kid favorite, and this sauce proves that you don't need heavy cream or complex ingredients to make something delicious. It's a lesson in Italian culinary philosophy: quality ingredients, simple technique.
