Our Foundation

The 7 Pillars of ChildsFood

Everything we build rests on these principles — from recipes to recommendations, from meal plans to cooking lessons.

🌎

Global Food Library

The most culturally diverse children's food library online. 200+ recipes from 50+ countries organized by region, culture, and season.

🍨

Smart Meal Engine

Input your child's age, diet, allergies, and budget. Get instant personalized meal plans with grocery lists and nutrition scores.

📈

Simple to Complex

Every meal has 3 layers: Simple (5 ingredients), Moderate (7-10 ingredients), and Kid Chef Mode (full recipe with plating).

🎨

Make It Theirs

Color customization, topping bars, plating creativity, and flavor swaps. Children design their own plates and build food agency.

🍅

Happy Plate

Visual balance instead of calorie counting. Protein zone, fiber zone, color diversity, hydration, and healthy fats. No shame, no numbers.

💜

Picky Eating Reframed

Food exposure ladders, texture progression, flavor bridges, and neutral language. Reduce stress, increase curiosity naturally.

👨‍🍳

Kid Cooking Academy

Structured cooking skill progression from age 3 to teens. Washing, stirring, knife skills, stovetop, meal planning, and mastery.

Kid Cooking Academy

From Kitchen Helper to Kid Chef

A structured cooking skill progression that builds confidence, independence, and lifelong healthy habits.

Ages 3–5

Little Kitchen Helper

Start with safe, fun tasks that build comfort in the kitchen and excitement about food.

🧋 Washing produce 🌰 Stirring ingredients 🥗 Tearing greens 🦸 Mashing bananas
Ages 6–8

Junior Chef

Build real cooking skills with safe tools and measured independence.

📏 Measuring ingredients 🔪 Safe knife skills 🥣 Mixing sauces 🍴 Setting the table
Ages 9–12

Rising Chef

Tackle real cooking techniques and create complete meals with growing confidence.

🍳 Stovetop basics 🧂 Seasoning & spices 🥑 Simple full meals 🍴 Following recipes
Teens 13+

Master Chef

Full food independence: planning, shopping, cooking, and mastering cultural recipes.

📋 Meal planning 🛒 Budget shopping 🥦 Batch cooking 🌎 Cultural recipe mastery
