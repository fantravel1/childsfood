🌊 Mediterranean Cuisine

Fresh, bright, simple. The foods of Greece, Italy, Spain, and beyond celebrate sunshine, olive oil, and fresh ingredients.

The Mediterranean Way

Simplicity: Let quality ingredients shine. A ripe tomato, fresh basil, good olive oil—that's a meal.

Seasonality: Food changes with the seasons. Spring vegetables, summer tomatoes, autumn figs, winter greens.

Healthfulness: The Mediterranean diet is one of the world's healthiest. Vegetables, whole grains, olive oil, fish.

Key Ingredients

  • 🫓 Olive oil (the liquid gold)
  • 🍅 Tomatoes (fresh and in sauces)
  • 🧄 Garlic & onions
  • 🌿 Fresh herbs (basil, oregano, thyme)
  • 🐟 Fresh fish & seafood
  • 🥑 Olives & capers
  • 🧀 Feta, mozzarella, hard cheeses
  • 🥗 Leafy greens & vegetables
