🏜️ Middle Eastern Cuisine

Ancient wisdom, aromatic spices, and shared platters. Food as hospitality, celebration, and connection.

The Heart of Hospitality

In Middle Eastern culture, hospitality is expressed through food. Serving guests generously, offering tea and treats—this is kindness.

Shared Plates: Food arrives on large platters meant for sharing. This builds community.

Spice Knowledge: Spices are used for flavor and health. Turmeric, cinnamon, cumin, and more have medicinal wisdom.

Signature Ingredients

  • 🫘 Chickpeas & hummus
  • 🌿 Fresh herbs (parsley, mint)
  • 🍯 Tahini & sesame
  • 🔶 Warm spices (cumin, cinnamon)
  • 🫓 Olive oil
  • 🍃 Za'atar spice blend
  • 🫓 Dates & dried fruits
  • 💚 Leafy greens

Values to Share

Middle Eastern food teaches children:

  • ✨ Hospitality is love expressed through food
  • 🫶 Sharing brings people together
  • 💪 Spices are powerful (flavor AND health)
  • 🌍 Ancient traditions have value
  • 🕊️ Generosity is a virtue
