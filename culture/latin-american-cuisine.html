🌎 Latin American Cuisine

Bold, vibrant, and diverse. From Aztec heritage to modern celebrations—food is culture, family, and joy.

The Foundation

The Trinity: Corn, beans, and chilies are the foundation of Latin American cooking. These three create complete proteins.

Pre-Columbian Wisdom: Tomatoes, potatoes, chocolate, vanilla, squash—many foods originated in the Americas.

Family & Celebration: Food is how Latin American culture celebrates. Birthdays, holidays, gatherings are food-centered.

Key Staples

  • 🌽 Corn (fresh, tortillas, masa)
  • 🫘 Beans (black, pinto, refried)
  • 🌶️ Chilies (mild to very spicy)
  • 🍅 Tomatoes & tomatillos
  • 🥑 Lime & citrus
  • 🥑 Avocados
  • 🍠 Potatoes & sweet potatoes
  • 🥬 Cilantro & fresh herbs

Regional Highlights

Mexico: Chilies, moles, fresh ingredients, corn in every form.

Brazil: Beans + rice, tropical fruits, African-influenced flavors.

Peru: Potatoes (their gift to the world!), quinoa, coastal seafood.

Central America: Corn-based, beans, tropical fruits, fresh.
