What is the Happy Plate?

The Happy Plate Framework teaches children (and parents!) to build balanced meals visually. Instead of counting calories or macros, we look at the plate and ask: "Does this plate have all the colors and groups we need?"

This approach is joyful, positive, and teaches kids how to feed themselves well for life.

How to Teach the Happy Plate

Make it Visual: Don't talk numbers. Say: "Let's make a rainbow plate!" or "Can you find a green veggie?"

Make it Fun: "We're building a happy plate together. What colors should we add?"

Make it Positive: Never shame. Instead: "Wow, you chose 4 colors today! Your body is happy!"

Make it Flexible: Some days a plate is a sandwich + fruit. Some days it's a bowl. What matters is the balance, not the presentation.
