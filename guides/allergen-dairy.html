Avoiding dairy while meeting calcium & nutrition needs.

What to Avoid

Obvious dairy: Milk, cheese, yogurt, butter, cream, ice cream, condensed milk.

Hidden dairy (read labels!): Whey, casein, milk solids, lactalbumin, lactoglobulin, ghee, milk fat, milk protein concentrate, curds.

Often contains dairy: Baked goods, chocolate, salad dressings, processed meats, margarine, non-dairy creamers (many contain casein!), protein bars.

Calcium Sources (Without Dairy)

Kids still need 500-1,300mg calcium daily. Here's where to get it:

  • Fortified plant milks: 300mg per cup (check label!)
  • Leafy greens: Collards, kale, bok choy, Chinese broccoli (200-300mg per cup)
  • Legumes: White beans, chickpeas (100-200mg per cup)
  • Tofu: Especially calcium-set (200-400mg per serving)
  • Fish with bones: Canned salmon, sardines (200mg per serving)
  • Seeds: Sesame, chia (150-250mg per 3 tablespoons)
  • Dried figs: 100mg per 5 figs
  • Almonds: 80mg per ounce
