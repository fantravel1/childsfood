The Truth About Budget Eating

Healthy eating on a budget is possible. It requires planning, not expensive ingredients. Dried beans cost less than junk food. Frozen vegetables are better than soda. Real food is always cheaper than processed food when you do the math.

You are not failing if money is tight. This is about feeding your family with what you have, with dignity and nutrition.
